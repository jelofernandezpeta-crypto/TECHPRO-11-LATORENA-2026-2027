VISAYAS
                       
SAN JUANICO BRIDGE SAMBAWAN ISLAND CHOCOLATE HILLS BOHOL KALANGGAMAN ISLAND PANAY ISLAND
The San Juanico Bridge is a 2.16-kilometer (1.34 mi) arch-shaped truss bridge stretching across the San Juanico Strait to connect the islands of Samar and Leyte Sambawan Island is a crescent-shaped paradise located off the coast of Maripipi, Biliran in the Philippines. Panay is a major triangular island in the Western Visayas. Spanning 11,809 sq. km, it comprises four provinces: Aklan, Antique, Capiz, and Iloilo.

The Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah Mosque—widely known as the Grand Mosque of Cotabato—is the second-largest mosque in the Philippines.
